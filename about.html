Heritage

Nature, Refined.

In the remote North of England, where the wind bites and the rain shapes the stone, lies the Hepple Estate. This is a landscape of profound silence and sudden, explosive growth. It is here that we have chosen to build our distillery, not for convenience, but for proximity to the wild.

The Three-Stage Method

Most gins are made once. Hepple is made three times. We use copper pot distillation for the classic heart, vacuum distillation for the delicate botanicals like Douglas Fir, and CO2 extraction for the pure, resinous oil of the juniper. This allows us to layer flavors in a way that traditional methods simply cannot achieve.
